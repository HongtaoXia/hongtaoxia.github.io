About

Engineering vision systems that work beyond the lab.

I am a Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at Henan University of Technology. My work combines mechanical design, industrial imaging, lightweight deep learning, embedded control, and software development.

I am particularly interested in robust perception and intelligent equipment for agriculture and postharvest systems. I also enjoy robotics research involving simulation, kinematics, and integrated robot systems.

Master's research · Computer vision

Online wheat impurity monitoring during postharvest cleaning

Developed a lightweight mechanical–visual inspection system for continuous-flow wheat cleaning. The work integrates controlled feeding and vibration-based dispersion, industrial imaging, a lightweight detector, residence-time-corrected counting, and CPU-only edge inference.
